I tend to do my own graphic designs and i'm a huge fan of Web Design (UI/UX experience). My favorite hobbies are hiking and playing video games. I like to work on my own but I have worked on projects with others. Right now i'm working as a freelance Web Developer at Glowbright Studios, a company I founded.
